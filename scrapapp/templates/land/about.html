About Scrapify

Scrapify is built to make recycling smarter and more impactful. We connect households, scrap dealers, and admins with a smooth platform that handles everything — from pickups to rate management.

Our mission is to reduce waste, promote sustainability, and empower local communities by turning recyclable materials into valuable resources. With real-time tracking, transparent pricing, and eco-friendly workflows, we ensure a trusted and efficient recycling network.
